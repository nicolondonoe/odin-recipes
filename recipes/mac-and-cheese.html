Mac & Cheese

Description

This slow cooker mac and cheese is creamy, comforting, and takes just moments to assemble in a slow cooker. Great for large family gatherings and to take to potluck dinners. It's always a big hit!

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package elbow macaroni
  • ½ cup butter
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1 (5 ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 2 large eggs, well beaten
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed Cheddar cheese soup (such as Campbell's)
  • 1 pinch paprika, or as desired (Optional)

Steps:

  1. Gather all your ingredients
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in macaroni pasta and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain and transfer pasta to a slow cooker.
  4. Stir butter into pasta until melted; season with salt and black pepper. Sprinkle about ½ Cheddar cheese over pasta; stir.
  5. Whisk evaporated milk and eggs together in a bowl until smooth; stir into pasta mixture.
  6. Whisk whole milk and condensed soup together in a bowl until smooth; stir into pasta mixture.
  7. Sprinkle remaining ½ Cheddar cheese over pasta mixture; sprinkle with paprika.
  8. Cook on Low for 3 hours, checking the edges are not getting too brown after 2 ½ hours.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
